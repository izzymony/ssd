About us

Penpot is the web-based open-source design tool that bridges the gap between designers and developers. Our mission is to provide an open-source & open-standards platform to bring collaboration between designers and developers to the next level. Kaleidos Open Source is Penpot's mother company — a tech startup, founded in 2011, focused on generating positive impact in society through open-source software and innovation. Kaleidos has raised enough funds via its VCs to support the project and to ensure there is an amazing multidisciplinary team focused on Penpot, also able to engage with our enthusiastic community of users and contributors.
